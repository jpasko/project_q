Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up a custom domain name for my portfolio?

In order to use a custom domain name, you must first be a paying user. If you would like to upgrade your account, please go here.

Once you're a paying user, you can proceed. Here are the steps:

  1. Purchase a domain name from a domain registrar such as NameCheap or GoDaddy. You can also use a domain name you already own.

  2. Create the following records within your domain registrar. This example is taken from NameCheap:

    Host Name IP Address/URL Record Type
    @ http://www.yourdomainname.com/ URL Redirect
    www domains.folio24.com. CNAME


    The URL Redirect record may already exist. Ensure that the CNAME record exists and hit "save".

  3. Check that you entered the records correctly. To do this, type yourdomainname.com (without the "www") into your browser's address bar. A generic "not found" page should come up, depending on your browser. Verify that "www.yourdomainname.com" appears in the address bar (with the "www"). You may have to wait up to 24 hours for DNS records to propagate.

  4. Under the "tools" menu of your portfolio, click "Custom domain".

  5. In the box that pops up, enter your domain (e.g. yourdomainname.com). You must not enter "www" or anything other than yourdomainname.com.

  6. Click "save". You may have to wait up to 48 hours for the full changes to take effect.

    If you've waited and your domain still doesn't work, double-check that you entered your domain name correctly and that you added the CNAME record as stated above.

    Feel free to contact us for support.

NOTE: If you log out and visit your portfolio while your new domain name is still being processed, you should see a "Not Found" page. You should also see www.yourdomainname.com in the address bar.

NOTE: You can still access your portfolio during this stage by signing in.

My portfolio isn't working correctly. My edits aren't saved and it looks weird. What gives?

If you're using an old browser, there is likely a lack of support for your setup. Another possibility is that you've disabled javascript, which is required for Folio24 to work properly.

To fix these issues, upgrade to the latest version of Chrome (preferred) or Firefox; your portfolio should work and you'll be doing the internet a huge favor! In addition, many other sites will work more effectively, your computer will be more secure, and web developers everywhere will thank you.

I ran out of uploads. What are my options?

  • You can obtain additional uploads by upgrading your account.
  • If you don't want to upgrade your account, you must delete uploads to make room for others.
  • If you already have a professional account and need even more uploads, please contact us.

How do I upload an image or video?

Simply click on the gallery you'd like to upload an image or video into. Then click on the "Upload Image" or "Upload Video" button.

How do I change the order of my images, videos, or galleries?

You can drag and drop galleries, images, or videos to rearrange them. Your changes will be stored automatically.

What is Google Analytics?

Google Analytics is a free tool that allows you to track how many times you profile has been viewed, along with other statistics. It can be very useful if you're curious or concerned about how often people view your work.

How do I enable Google Analytics?

  1. Create an account with Google Analytics.
  2. Add your portfolio's URL (http://{{ username }}.{{ DOMAIN }} or http://www.yourcustomdomain.com) to your Google Analytics account's web property setting.
  3. Find your user account number from the Admin tab in your Google Analytics account. It should look something like UA-12345-1.
  4. Go to your portfolio's customize page and enter your Google Analytics account number.

Your portfolio should now have Google Analytics enabled. You can track your portfolio's statistics using your Google Analytics account.

How do I edit my About page?

Go to your about page and click on any of the headings to edit its content. For example, if you want to edit the "Location" part of your profile, simply click on "Location" -- this will open an inline editor. Enter your location and click save. You can edit the other fields in a similar manner.

Note that fields which display Currently Empty will not be shown publicly.

You can also upload, change, or delete your profile picture.

How do I enable or disable my Blog or Contact page?

  • Enable your Blog by entering its URL in the customize form. Disable it by clearing its URL in the form.
  • Your Contact form is enabled by default. You can change its settings within the customize form as well.
